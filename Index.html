OUR SPECIALITIES

Breakfast

Morning at COX CRAB promises a delightful marine symphony to awaken your senses. With 10 unique breakfast options, our patrons can relish everything from hearty crab Benedict to delicate seafood crepes. Begin your day with an oceanic flourish only at COX CRAB.

Lunch

Experience a midday seafood retreat with COX CRAB’s exclusive lunch offerings. Boasting 11 curated dishes, our lunch menu ranges from the classic shrimp po'boy to the innovative seaweed salad bowl. Take a break from the ordinary and let your lunchtime be a seafood escapade.

Snacks & Desserts

Every bite from our snack menu is a testament to COX CRAB’s culinary excellence. Offering 13 types, we have treats like crispy calamari rings and tangy oyster shooters. Perfect for those mid-afternoon cravings or early evening appetizers, our snacks are sure to tantalize your taste buds.

Dinner

Dining under the ambient lights of COX CRAB is an experience like no other. Our extensive dinner menu, featuring 15 items, is a journey through the ocean's finest treasures. Whether it's our lobster thermidor or the exotic fish curry, each dish promises a memorable dining moment.

Beverages

Quench your thirst with COX CRAB's distinctive range of beverages. With 6 items to choose from, our bar crafts marine-inspired cocktails, fresh mocktails, and an array of other drinks. From the seaweed-infused gin and tonic to our refreshing blue lagoon lemonade, every sip complements our seafood marvels.

Always Open For You

When hunger strikes, time shouldn't be a barrier. Day or night, rain or shine, COX CRAB welcomes you with open doors. Dive into your favorites, anytime the appetite calls. At COX CRAB, we understand that cravings have no time limit. That's why we're open 24/7, ensuring you can enjoy our delicious offerings anytime you desire.
